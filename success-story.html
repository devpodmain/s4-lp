How Decision Minds Rebuilt Our Intelligence Platform

Using Claude and an agentic approach, Decision Minds drastically simplified our customer success complexity, turning robust workflows into sleek, automated processes.

The Challenge

Our original platform suffered from fragmented data silos. Information was scattered across multiple CRM, support, and marketing tools. Building a unified view required complex data engineering that was slow to adapt.

The Agentic Solution with Claude

Decision Minds integrated Claude to intelligently parse and structure unstructured data streams in real-time. By deploying agents at critical data junctions, the platform now automatically understands intent, predicts churn, and maps out actionable customer success playbooks without human intervention.

Impact on Productivity (Months 1-6)

30% M1
50% M3
90% M6

Results

Within 6 months, we saw a 40% reduction in churn and saved over $2.5M in retained revenue. The complex engineering tasks that once took weeks can now be customized via simple prompt commands, revolutionizing how the customer success team operates.
